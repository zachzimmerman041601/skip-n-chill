How it Works

Set it and forget it.

Skip 'n Chill runs silently in the background. No browser extensions, no complicated setup. Just launch it and enjoy your videos.

1

Launch the app

Open Skip 'n Chill and let it run in the background. It sits quietly in your system tray, ready to go.

2

It watches your screen

The app continuously monitors your display for the YouTube "Skip Ad" button using lightweight screen detection.

3

Ads get skipped instantly

The moment a skippable ad appears, Skip 'n Chill clicks the button for you. You barely notice the interruption.
